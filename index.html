Francisco Goya

[GOYA Y LUCIENTES, FRANCISCO DE.] L'Art: Revue Hebdomadaire Illustrée. Paris: A. Ballue, 1876-1881. 11 volumes. Illustrated with numerous engraved, etched, and lithographed plates, including 4 etchings with aquatint (240 x 350 mm) by Goya in Troisiéme Année, Tome II, 1877. Folio (440 x 305 mm). Half roan, giltstamped, binding defective. With five additional volumes, art history. The first publication of four plates by Goya: "Lluvia de Toros," "Otras Leyes Por El Pueblo," "Que Guerrero," and "Una Reina del Circo." Part of the group of 22 etchings known as "Los Disparates" or "Los Proverbios," of which 18 had previously been published in 1864. Harris 266-9. Delteil 220-3.
